# Brand Diagnosis Analyzer

A Claude AI skill for comprehensive brand diagnosis and growth breakthrough analysis, based on 23 years of brand marketing experience.

## 🎯 What This Skill Does

**"Sales stagnation isn't a product problem - it's a strategy problem."**

This skill helps you diagnose brand issues from multiple dimensions: product power, brand power, channel efficiency, and communication effectiveness, then provides actionable solutions for breakthrough growth.

## 🔍 The Four-Dimension Diagnosis Framework

### Dimension 1: Product Power (产品力)
Is your product genuinely competitive?
- Functionality & Quality
- Value Proposition
- Differentiation

### Dimension 2: Brand Power (品牌力)
Does your brand have equity?
- Brand Awareness
- Brand Loyalty
- Brand Association

### Dimension 3: Channel Power (渠道力)
Can customers find you?
- Distribution Coverage
- Channel Efficiency
- Channel Optimization

### Dimension 4: Communication Power (传播力)
Do you speak effectively?
- Media Strategy
- Content Quality
- Engagement Rate

## 📊 Analysis Output

The skill provides a structured diagnosis report with:
1. Current situation assessment
2. Root cause analysis
3. Growth breakthrough points identification
4. Actionable recommendations

## 💼 Use Cases

| Scenario | Application |
|---|---|
| Sales Plateau | Identify hidden growth barriers |
| New Product Launch | Define competitive positioning |
| Brand Refresh | Diagnose equity gaps |
| Channel Optimization | Improve distribution efficiency |
| Marketing Strategy | Optimize media investment |

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Provide brand/product information and current challenges
2. The skill applies the four-dimension framework for diagnosis
3. Receive structured recommendations for breakthrough growth
