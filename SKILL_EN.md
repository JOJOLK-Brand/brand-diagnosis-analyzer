# Brand Diagnosis Analyzer

A professional skill for comprehensive brand diagnosis and growth breakthrough analysis.

## Overview

When sales plateau, the problem is rarely the product itself—it's usually a gap in one or more of the four critical dimensions: product power, brand power, channel power, or communication power.

This skill helps you:
1. Diagnose which dimension is causing the bottleneck
2. Identify the specific "growth killer" within that dimension
3. Provide actionable recommendations for breakthrough

## The Four-Dimension Framework

### 1. Product Power (产品力)
Questions to diagnose:
- Is the core function competitive?
- Is value proposition clear?
- Is there clear differentiation?

### 2. Brand Power (品牌力)
Questions to diagnose:
- Is brand awareness sufficient?
- Is there emotional connection?
- Is brand loyalty strong?

### 3. Channel Power (渠道力)
Questions to diagnose:
- Is distribution wide enough?
- Are channels efficient?
- Is there channel conflict?

### 4. Communication Power (传播力)
Questions to diagnose:
- Is messaging resonant?
- Is media selection effective?
- Is content engaging?

## Diagnostic Process

1. **Situation Assessment**: Gather current market data
2. **Dimension Analysis**: Score each of the four dimensions
3. **Root Cause Identification**: Find the bottleneck dimension
4. **Breakthrough Point**: Identify specific opportunity
5. **Action Plan**: Develop actionable recommendations
